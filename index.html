How It Works

in 2 weeks.

A clear, structured process with full transparency at every step. You'll always know where your app is at.

01

Discovery Sprint

We spend week one deep-diving into your coaching method, target clients, and business goals. We map every screen, every flow, every feature before a single line of code is written.
